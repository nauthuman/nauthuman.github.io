Cullen Gray

Director (sample 3)

Cullen grew up just south of the infamous Black Hills Forest where the Blair Witch stalks the woods. He fell in love with all kinds of film and theatre at a young age, but an early encounter with Guillermo del Toro’s Pan’s Labyrinth left him with a taste for all things dark and fantastical. He’s worked as an actor, writer, director, and producer for both the stage and the screen since moving to Atlanta in 2018, and he’s currently the Creative Director and Founder of Naut Human Productions.
