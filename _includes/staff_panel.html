Welcome to our hosts Steve Chen and Casey Watts!

Steve is heavily involved in the DC and NYC tech meetup community. In DC, he co-founded DC Code & Coffee, hosted groups like NodeDC & DCJS, and now runs ReactDC and DC Product & Pastries. In NYC and beyond, he founded and operates as the Community Executive Officer of the Code & Coffee organization, a series of community-led meetups in 21 US cities and growing! He has even been recognized by Meetup for his work with NYC Code & Coffee. Previously he was the Head of Developer Relations at Openbase, and an early engineer at DC startups, Framebridge and Arcadia.
